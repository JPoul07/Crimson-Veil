About us

We are a metal Trio consisting of our bassist Jerry, our guitarist Xavier and our drummer and vocalist, Jeremy. We are situated in the Gatineau/Ottawa region. you can read about us more just bellow. Also take the time to go look at our equipement to see if you'd want to buy the same as us.

Jerry:

Jerry learned to play the bass when he was only five years old. He's been touring with some of the biggest bands but now wants to go with his town's local band to be closer to home.

Xavier:

The second that Xavier touches a guitar, the sounds that come out are immaculate. He's wanted to join a band for an eternity so as soon as he got the call for the band he was directly on board

Jeremy:

Jeremy has always had a flair for the audience. At age 10 he was already drumming with his dad's friend. Already aspiring to when he was going to be able to tour the world. He dedicates all his work to his late granduncle.
