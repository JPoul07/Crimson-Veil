About us

We are a metal group consisted of 5 members. Robert on the bass, Kirk on the lead guitar, Vinnie on the drums, Hailey on percussion and vocals and finally Jeremy on the rhythm guitar and vocals. We are close-knit crew that managed to create a decent following in the Quebec/Ontario region.
